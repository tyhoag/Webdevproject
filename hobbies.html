Why You Should Climb

Rock climbing is the only sport that demands your full attention — you simply cannot think about anything else when the next hold is all that stands between you and the ground. It's problem-solving under pressure, executed with your whole body.

It's also one of the most social sports out there. Every crag has a community. You belay strangers, swap beta, and cheer people through cruxes you've already done. If you're looking for a reason to start — this is it.
